About Me

I am a student at Rochester Institute of Technology working towards a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering with a minor in Computer Science. I have completed two years of my degree and currently I am interning with Amazon in Cambridge, Massachusetts as a Software Development Engineer. There are several small projects on this website, some of which have links to the code on GitHub, that showcase personal projects and things I have done for college courses.

I acheived my Eagle Rank in July of 2015 after my senior year of high school. The Eagle Rank is the highest rank in Boy Scouts that is only achieved by about 5% of all Boy Scouts. Obtaining Eagle Rank also allows anyone who joins the military to start at the rank of E-2 instead of E-1. In June of 2015 I graduated from Liverpool High School and began my college carrer in Software Engineering.

My plans for the future include completing my undergraduate degree at RIT, gaining experience as a professional Software Engineer through internships/Co-ops, and eventually getting a graduate degree in Computer Science or a related-field to advance my knowledge in computing.

To learn more about my experience in computing look at the Projects page of this website and to learn more about my skills in general, take a look at my resume through the link on the Resume page of this website.
